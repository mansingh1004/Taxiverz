About Raxaul to Janakpur Taxi Service

Taxiverz offers dedicated cross-border taxi services from Raxaul to Janakpur, the sacred birthplace of Goddess Sita. Covering approximately 80 kilometers, this short but spiritually significant journey connects you to one of Hinduism's most revered pilgrimage sites. Our experienced drivers ensure smooth border crossing and comfortable travel to this holy city.

The journey from Raxaul to Janakpur takes around 2-3 hours including border formalities at Birgunj. This is one of the shortest and most convenient routes for pilgrims visiting Janakpur from India. Our professional drivers are familiar with the route and border procedures, ensuring a hassle-free pilgrimage experience.

Janakpur, famous for the Janaki Mandir dedicated to Goddess Sita, attracts thousands of devotees throughout the year. Whether you're visiting for darshan, attending religious ceremonies, or exploring the city's rich Ramayana heritage, our taxi service provides convenient and respectful transportation for your spiritual journey.

Our vehicles are well-maintained and equipped for cross-border travel with all necessary permits. We handle border crossing efficiently and provide guidance on immigration procedures. Our transparent pricing includes all border charges and tolls, ensuring no surprises during your pilgrimage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does it take from Raxaul to Janakpur?

The journey takes approximately 2-3 hours including border crossing time at Birgunj. Very convenient for day trips.

Q2: What documents are needed?

Indian citizens need valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Passport). No visa required for Nepal.

Q3: Can you wait during temple darshan?

Yes, we wait while you complete your darshan and prayers. Our drivers are familiar with temple timings and can guide you.

Q4: Is same-day return possible?

Yes, same-day return trips are very popular. We offer round-trip packages at economical rates.

Q5: Can we visit other temples in Janakpur?

Yes, we can arrange visits to Ram Mandir, Dhanush Sagar, and other important religious sites in Janakpur.
