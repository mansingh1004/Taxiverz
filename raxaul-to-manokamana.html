About Raxaul to Manokamana Taxi Service

Taxiverz offers dedicated cross-border taxi services from Raxaul to Manokamana, one of Nepal's most revered temples dedicated to Goddess Bhagwati. Covering approximately 250 kilometers, this spiritual journey takes you from the India-Nepal border to the sacred shrine accessible by cable car. Our experienced drivers ensure smooth border crossing and comfortable travel through Nepal's scenic landscapes.

The journey from Raxaul to Manokamana takes around 7-8 hours including border formalities at Birgunj. The route passes through Kathmandu valley and requires some mountain driving expertise. Our professional drivers are trained in hill driving and familiar with the route, ensuring your safety and comfort throughout this spiritual journey.

Manokamana temple, meaning "wish-fulfilling goddess," is believed to grant the wishes of sincere devotees. The temple is accessible via a scenic cable car ride offering breathtaking Himalayan views. Whether you're visiting for darshan, religious ceremonies, or experiencing Nepal's spiritual heritage, our taxi service provides convenient and respectful transportation.

Our vehicles are well-maintained and suitable for international travel with all necessary permits and insurance. We handle border procedures efficiently and provide guidance on visa requirements, currency exchange, and cable car timings. Our transparent pricing includes all border charges and tolls for this cross-border pilgrimage.

Book your Raxaul to Manokamana taxi with Taxiverz for a blessed pilgrimage. Our drivers understand the religious significance of your journey and ensure comfortable, timely service. Choose us for reliable cross-border taxi services that respect the sanctity of your visit to Manokamana Devi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does it take from Raxaul to Manokamana?

The journey takes approximately 7-8 hours including border crossing. We drop you at the cable car station.

Q2: Is cable car fare included?

Cable car tickets are not included in taxi fare. We wait at the station while you visit the temple and return.

Q3: What documents do I need?

Indian citizens need valid ID proof (Aadhar, Voter ID, or Passport). No visa required for Nepal.

Q4: Can we visit other nearby temples?

Yes, we can arrange visits to nearby temples and attractions. Our drivers are familiar with the area.

Q5: Is same-day return possible?

Yes, same-day return is possible but will be a long day. We recommend starting early morning for comfortable travel.
