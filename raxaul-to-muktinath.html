About Raxaul to Muktinath Taxi Service

Taxiverz offers specialized cross-border taxi services from Raxaul to Muktinath, one of the highest and most sacred temples in Nepal. Covering approximately 450 kilometers, this challenging yet spiritually rewarding journey takes you from the plains to the Himalayan heights. Our experienced drivers are trained in mountain driving and ensure a safe, comfortable pilgrimage to this revered shrine.

The journey from Raxaul to Muktinath takes around 12-14 hours via Kathmandu and Pokhara, with the final stretch involving high-altitude mountain roads. We strongly recommend breaking this journey with overnight stops for acclimatization and rest. Our professional drivers are familiar with mountain conditions and ensure your safety throughout this challenging pilgrimage.

Muktinath temple, located at 3,710 meters in the Mustang district, is sacred to both Hindus and Buddhists. The temple is famous for its 108 water spouts and eternal flame. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does it take from Raxaul to Muktinath?

The journey takes 12-14 hours of driving. We strongly recommend 2-3 days with overnight stops for acclimatization and safety.

Q2: Do I need special permits?

Yes, TIMS card and Annapurna Conservation Area Permit (ACAP) are required. We can guide you on obtaining these permits.

Q3: What about altitude sickness?

We recommend gradual ascent with overnight stops. Carry altitude sickness medication and consult your doctor before travel.

Q4: What should I carry for this journey?

Carry warm clothing, medicines, ID proof, and cash. Weather can be unpredictable at high altitudes.

Q5: Is the route safe year-round?

Best time is March-May and September-November. Winter months can have snow and road closures. Check weather before booking.
