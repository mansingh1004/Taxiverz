Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does it take from Raxaul to Kathmandu?

The journey takes 4-5 hours including border crossing time. We ensure efficient processing to minimize waiting time at immigration.

Q2: What documents do I need for border crossing?

Indian citizens need valid ID proof (Aadhar, Voter ID, or Passport). Our drivers assist with all border formalities.

Q3: Can you pick me up from Raxaul railway station?

Yes, we provide pickup services from Raxaul railway station, bus stand, or any location in Raxaul town.

Q4: Is currency exchange available at the border?

Yes, authorized money changers are available at Birgunj border. We can guide you to get the best exchange rates.

Q5: Are there any additional charges at the border?

All border charges and tolls are included in our quoted fare. No hidden charges or surprises.
