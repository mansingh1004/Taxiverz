About Raxaul to Chitwan Taxi Service

Taxiverz offers specialized cross-border taxi services from Raxaul to Chitwan National Park, Nepal's premier wildlife destination. Covering approximately 180 kilometers, this journey takes you from the India-Nepal border to one of Asia's finest wildlife reserves. Our experienced drivers ensure smooth border crossing and comfortable travel through Nepal's Terai region.

The journey from Raxaul to Chitwan takes around 5-6 hours including border formalities at Birgunj. The route passes through scenic landscapes and rural Nepal, offering glimpses of local life. Our professional drivers are familiar with the route and ensure a safe, comfortable journey to this wildlife paradise.

Chitwan National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is home to one-horned rhinoceros, Bengal tigers, and diverse wildlife. Whether you're visiting for jungle safari, bird watching, or nature photography, our taxi service provides convenient transportation with the flexibility to accommodate your wildlife adventure schedule.

Our vehicles are well-maintained and suitable for international travel with all necessary permits and insurance. We handle border procedures efficiently and provide guidance on park entry, accommodation options, and safari bookings. Our transparent pricing includes all border charges and tolls for this cross-border journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does it take from Raxaul to Chitwan?

The journey takes approximately 5-6 hours including border crossing at Birgunj. Perfect for reaching Chitwan in time for evening activities.

Q2: Can you help with safari bookings?

While we don't book safaris directly, our drivers can guide you to reliable tour operators and resorts in Chitwan.

Q3: What documents are needed for Nepal?

Indian citizens need valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Passport). No visa required for Nepal.

Q4: Can you wait for multi-day trips?

Yes, we offer multi-day packages where the vehicle and driver stay with you. Ideal for Chitwan jungle safari packages.

Q5: Is luggage space available for camping gear?

Yes, our SUVs have ample space for luggage, camping gear, and photography equipment.
