Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does it take to reach Manokamana from Gorakhpur?

The journey takes approximately 8-9 hours including border crossing time. We ensure efficient processing at immigration.

Q2: Do I need a visa for Nepal?

Indian citizens don't need a visa. Carry valid ID proof like Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Passport for border crossing.

Q3: Is the cable car ride to temple included?

Cable car tickets are not included in taxi fare. We drop you at the cable car station and wait for your return.

Q4: Can we visit other temples nearby?

Yes, we can arrange visits to nearby temples and attractions. Our drivers are familiar with the area and can guide you.

Q5: Is currency exchange available?

Yes, currency exchange facilities are available at the border. We guide you to authorized changers for best rates.
