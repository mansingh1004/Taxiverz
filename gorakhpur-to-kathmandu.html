Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Do I need a visa to travel to Kathmandu?

Indian citizens don't need a visa for Nepal. However, carry a valid ID proof like Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Passport for border crossing.

Q2: How long does border clearance take?

Border clearance typically takes 30-60 minutes. Our drivers assist with all formalities to ensure smooth and quick processing.

Q3: Can I exchange currency during the journey?

Yes, currency exchange facilities are available at the border. We can guide you to authorized money changers for best rates.

Q4: Are there any restrictions on luggage?

No specific restrictions for personal luggage. However, commercial goods may require customs declaration at the border.

Q5: Is the vehicle insured for Nepal travel?

Yes, all our vehicles have valid insurance covering both India and Nepal. Your safety is our priority.
